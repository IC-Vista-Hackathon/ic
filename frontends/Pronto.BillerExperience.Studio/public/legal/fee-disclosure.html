Pronto Fee Disclosure

Placeholder document for the Pronto demo. Actual fees are configured per biller and shown at checkout.

Service fees

A service fee may be applied to your payment to cover processing costs. The exact fee for your payment is displayed at checkout before you authorize it.

How fees are shown

The checkout summary itemizes your bill amount, any service fee, and the total you will be charged. You can review these amounts before confirming.

Fee changes

Fees may vary by payment method and by biller. Any change to the fee applied to your payment will be reflected in the checkout summary.
